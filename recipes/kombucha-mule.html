Kombucha Mule Cocktail

Description

This tart and fizzy version of the Moscow Mule uses ginger-flavored kombucha instead of ginger beer for a less sweet alternative

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Lime Juice
  • Vodka
  • Ginger Flavoured Kombucha
  • 1 Thin lime slice

Steps

  1. Fill copper mule mug with ice
  2. Add lime juice and vodka
  3. Stir well
  4. Add ginger kombucha
  5. Garnish with lime slice
