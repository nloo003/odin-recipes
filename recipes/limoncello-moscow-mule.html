Limoncello Moscow Mule

Description

TThis is a fun twist on the classic Moscow Mule, using limoncello for a refreshing, lemony summertime cocktail. Use a good-quality ginger beer, such as Fever TreeÂ®.

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Lime Juice
  • Vodka
  • Ginger Beer
  • Limoncello Liqueur
  • 1 Thin lemon slice

Steps

  1. Fill copper mule mug with ice
  2. Add lime juice,limoncello and vodka
  3. Stir well
  4. Add ginger beer
  5. Garnish with lemon slice
